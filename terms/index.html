Per-recording minute charges

  • Cancelling a recording before transcription completes is free. If you cancel during capture, while uploading, or while transcription is still in flight, no minute is metered against your balance.

This is intentional. The per-minute model is honest: when work happens, you pay for it. When work doesn’t happen, you don’t.
